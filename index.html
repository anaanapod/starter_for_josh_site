My name is Josh Whitkin. I'm a professional with more than 7 years industry experience in Games Design, Level Design, Scripting and Visual Effects. What makes me passionate about games is the combination of technology and creativity, and how multiple disciplines come together to create and interactive experince. Whether I'm designing for puzzle games, 2D platforms or 3D environments. I always tell a story.
